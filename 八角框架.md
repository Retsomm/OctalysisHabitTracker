要設計一款讓使用者「不需要依靠意志力」就能持續使用的目標 APP，你需要將設計核心從傳統的「功能導向設計」（Function-Focused Design）轉向**「以人為本的設計」（Human-Focused Design）**。[\[1\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=17&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A76.5%2C%22top%22%3A679.536%2C%22width%22%3A444.312%2C%22height%22%3A34.05600000000004%7D)

這種設計方法不只是追求效率，而是優先考慮人類的動機、情感與不安全感，將枯燥的任務轉化為有趣的體驗。[\[2\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=18&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A273.23999999999995%2C%22width%22%3A460.22400000000005%2C%22height%22%3A123.55200000000002%7D)以下是根據《遊戲化實戰全攻略》整理出你設計 APP 時必須掌握的八角框架（Octalysis）核心知識：

### 1\. 核心動機：八大驅動力的運用

八角框架認為，除非背後有這八種驅動力之一，否則使用者不會產生任何行為。[\[3\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=32&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A509.25600000000003%2C%22width%22%3A452.88%2C%22height%22%3A86.32799999999992%7D)[\[4\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=39&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A525.888%2C%22width%22%3A457.77599999999995%2C%22height%22%3A85.53599999999994%7D)

- **驅動力 1：史詩意義與使命感 (Epic Meaning & Calling)**：讓使用者覺得自己在做一件超越個人的大事。例如：在達成運動目標時，同時也在為某個慈善機構「捐贈」里程數。[\[5\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=33&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A154.836%2C%22top%22%3A108.504%2C%22width%22%3A299.88%2C%22height%22%3A19.799999999999997%7D)

- **驅動力 2：發展與成就感 (Development & Accomplishment)**：這是最常用的驅動力，透過克服挑戰、獲得勳章或獎盃來體現進步。[\[6\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=33&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A119.952%2C%22top%22%3A342.936%2C%22width%22%3A372.708%2C%22height%22%3A19.80000000000001%7D)

- **驅動力 3：創意授權與回饋 (Empowerment of Creativity & Feedback)**：讓使用者能自由嘗試不同路徑，並即時看到結果。例如：讓使用者自定義達成目標的策略，而不只是按表操課。[\[7\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=33&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A86.904%2C%22top%22%3A490.248%2C%22width%22%3A432.68399999999997%2C%22height%22%3A20.59200000000004%7D)

- **驅動力 4：所有權與佔有欲 (Ownership & Possession)**：使用者覺得「擁有」某個東西（如虛擬寵物、虛擬家園或累積的數據），就會想改進並增加它。[\[8\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=34&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A99.792%2C%22width%22%3A459%2C%22height%22%3A153.648%7D)

- **驅動力 5：社會影響力與關聯性 (Social Influence & Relatedness)**：利用同儕壓力、競爭、羨慕或師徒關係。例如：看到朋友達成目標，會激發使用者的動力。[\[9\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=34&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A299.376%2C%22width%22%3A457.164%2C%22height%22%3A138.60000000000008%7D)

- **驅動力 6：稀缺性與迫切感 (Scarcity & Impatience)**：因為得不到或需要等待，反而更想得到。例如：「每天僅限 100 名使用者領取的限時成就」。[\[10\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=34&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A160.95600000000002%2C%22top%22%3A453.816%2C%22width%22%3A287.64%2C%22height%22%3A19.80000000000001%7D)

- **驅動力 7：不確定性與好奇心 (Unpredictability & Curiosity)**：不知道下一步會發生什麼。例如：完成今日任務後可以「抽獎」獲得意外驚喜。[\[11\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=35&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A99.792%2C%22width%22%3A459%2C%22height%22%3A207.504%7D)

- **驅動力 8：損失與避免 (Loss & Avoidance)**：為了避免負面結果而行動。例如：如果今天不運動，好不容易累積的連勝紀錄就會消失。[\[12\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=35&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A352.44%2C%22width%22%3A457.77599999999995%2C%22height%22%3A137.808%7D)

### 2\. 動機的性質：大腦傾向與帽子分類

為了讓使用者不靠意志力，你需要平衡不同性質的驅動力：

- **左腦型 vs 右腦型驅動力**：

   - **左腦型 (外在動機)**：包含驅動力 2、4、6。使用者是為了獲得獎勵（目標、財富）而行動。注意：一旦停止獎勵，動機可能會驟降（過度辯護效應）。[\[13\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=36&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A336.59999999999997%2C%22width%22%3A454.10400000000004%2C%22height%22%3A102.9600000000001%7D)[\[14\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=36&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A657.36%2C%22width%22%3A460.22400000000005%2C%22height%22%3A51.48000000000002%7D)

   - **右腦型 (內在動機)**：包含驅動力 3、5、7。活動本身就是獎勵，不需要外在誘因。這類動機更持久。[\[15\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=36&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A539.3520000000001%2C%22width%22%3A457.164%2C%22height%22%3A102.95999999999992%7D)[\[16\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=37&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A154.44%2C%22width%22%3A447.98400000000004%2C%22height%22%3A68.90399999999997%7D)

- **白帽 vs 黑帽遊戲化**：

   - **白帽 (正面動機)**：頂部驅動力 (1, 2, 3)，讓人感到強大且有掌控感。[\[17\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=37&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A510.84000000000003%2C%22width%22%3A449.82000000000005%2C%22height%22%3A102.96000000000004%7D)

   - **黑帽 (負面動機)**：底部驅動力 (6, 7, 8)，讓人感到焦慮、著迷或成癮，雖然能產生短期爆發力，但長期可能讓使用者感到不舒服而離開。[\[18\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=37&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A629.64%2C%22width%22%3A454.10400000000004%2C%22height%22%3A87.12%7D)[\[19\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=38&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A71.28%2C%22width%22%3A460.22400000000005%2C%22height%22%3A51.480000000000004%7D)

### 3\. 使用者旅程的四個階段 (Level II)

你不能用同樣的方式去激勵第一天使用與第一百天使用的用戶，動機必須隨階段調整：[\[20\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=44&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A603.504%2C%22width%22%3A454.10400000000004%2C%22height%22%3A86.32799999999997%7D)

1. **發現 (Discovery)**：為什麼使用者想試用？通常與好奇心（驅動力 7）有關。[\[21\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=45&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A348.48%2C%22width%22%3A455.94000000000005%2C%22height%22%3A103.75199999999995%7D)

2. **新手上路 (Onboarding)**：如何讓使用者學會工具並感到自己很聰明（驅動力 2）？[\[22\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=45&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A467.28%2C%22width%22%3A459%2C%22height%22%3A120.38400000000001%7D)

3. **腳手架階段 (Scaffolding)**：日常重複的行為。此時社會影響力（驅動力 5）與追逐未完成目標（驅動力 6）最重要。[\[22\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=45&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A467.28%2C%22width%22%3A459%2C%22height%22%3A120.38400000000001%7D)

4. **終局 (Endgame)**：如何留住資深使用者？這通常依賴「不想失去成就」的損失避免（驅動力 8）。[\[22\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=45&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A467.28%2C%22width%22%3A459%2C%22height%22%3A120.38400000000001%7D)

### 4\. 實施方式：顯性 vs 隱性遊戲化

- **隱性遊戲化 (Implicit Gamification)**：這最適合目標 APP。將遊戲元素隱藏在用戶體驗中（如 LinkedIn 的進度條），使用者不會覺得自己在玩遊戲，但會不自覺地被驅動。[\[23\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=55&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A431.64000000000004%2C%22width%22%3A452.88%2C%22height%22%3A102.16799999999995%7D)[\[24\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=55&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A75.888%2C%22top%22%3A658.944%2C%22width%22%3A452.88%2C%22height%22%3A51.48000000000002%7D)

- **顯性遊戲化 (Explicit Gamification)**：使用者知道自己在玩遊戲（如透過玩 RPG 遊戲來完成健身任務），這更具創意但也可能讓部分專業人士感到幼稚。[\[25\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=54&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A254.232%2C%22width%22%3A449.20799999999997%2C%22height%22%3A87.11999999999998%7D)[\[26\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=55&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A176.616%2C%22width%22%3A455.94000000000005%2C%22height%22%3A190.08%7D)

### 設計建議總結

要實現「不靠意志力」，你的 APP 應該：

1. **從「白帽」切入**，建立使命感與成就感。

2. **強化「內在動機」（右腦型）**，讓制定計劃與社交互動本身就變得有趣。

3. **適度加入「黑帽」元素**（如每日簽到、損失避免），增加短期的行為緊迫感。

4. **針對不同階段設計**，確保新手感到勝任，而老手有不願離開的價值感。[\[27\]](https://app.heptabase.com/aed2d564-e8e0-4308-a2af-1455849a1bf4/card/9ec469dc-8a98-4a01-80f5-c43efaef9cdd?pdfPage=45&pdfBoundingBox=%7B%22left%22%3A74.664%2C%22top%22%3A603.504%2C%22width%22%3A462.06000000000006%2C%22height%22%3A86.32799999999997%7D)
